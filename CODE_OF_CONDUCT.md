# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, focused, and inclusive collaboration environment.

Examples of expected behavior:
- constructive technical feedback
- good-faith discussion of tradeoffs
- accountability for mistakes and fixes

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory language
- repeated disruptive behavior in issues or reviews

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct.
Repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent participation restrictions.
